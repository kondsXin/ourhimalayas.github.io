我是一個平凡的人
偶然間推開一扇門
那藍色海洋上的星辰
喧囂中把我指引

I am an ordinary person
Who happened to open a door
The stars above the azure ocean
Are guiding me through the noises and chaos

我是一個普通的人
多幸運變成了我們
在硝煙彌漫中追尋
正道與信仰長存

I am just an average person
How blessed I am to become one of us
As the flames of war raging across the world
We are pursuing the everlasting righteousness and faith

蒲公英在天空中飄蕩
讓夢想帶著希望去遠方
母親的淚光 依稀的過往
那思念 點亮了方向

Dandelions drifting across the sky
Let our prayers guide us in distant places and through difficult times
Mother’s tears and memories of the past
These sentiments light up our path

細雨中送來聲聲叮嚀啊
世間的冷暖也嘗過了吧
風中的問候你收到了嗎

The gentle rain brings mother’s messages from heaven
“Have you tasted the bitterness and sweetness of life?”
“Did you receive the love I sent through the breeze?”
The world is still full of divisions and strife
But in spite of it, we stand firm in our faith

戰火擊碎了 春日的暖陽

We are letting the world know that we are the extraordinary ones
Where liberty dwells, there is our home

我們都是漂泊的人
在異國凝聚成我們
把這時代托付的責任
寫進我們的青春

We are all drifters
From all around the world, we are united to become the New Federal State of China
We took on the responsibilities entrusted upon us in these times
And writing them into our youth and our lives

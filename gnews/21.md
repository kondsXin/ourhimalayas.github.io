马里乌波尔是南部港口城市，人口约 45 万。

Before the war, it’s strategically important linking Russian controlled parts of eastern Ukraine with a swath of territory the Kremlin has captured in the south.
战前，该市在连接俄罗斯控制的乌克兰东部地区与克里姆林宫在南部占领的大片领土方面具有重要的战略意义。

The International Criminal Courts definition of war crimes broadly include willfully killing, causing suffering or deliberately targeting civilians.
国际刑事法庭对战争罪的定义大致包括故意杀害、造成痛苦或故意针对平民。

Russia has denied using cluster bombs in Ukraine.
俄罗斯否认在乌克兰使用集束炸弹。

More than 100 countries have joined a treaty that bans such weapons though neither Ukraine nor Russia is a signatory.
尽管乌克兰和俄罗斯都不是签署国，但已有 100 多个国家加入了禁止此类武器的条约。

这些类型的弹药在飞行时会释放许多子弹药或小型炸弹，可对平民造成广泛的伤害，尤其在人口密集地区。

On March 1 Mariupol, city council posted photos of missile debris in a residential neighborhood.
